Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The missile man of India

Here is a timeline Dr Kalam's life:

  • 1931 - born on 15th October at Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1954 - Graduated from St. Joseph's college, Truchirappali
  • 1955 - Enrolled at the MIT to study aerospace engineering
  • 1960 - Joined DRDO as Chief Scientist
  • 1965 - Started work rocket project independently at DRDO
  • 1969 - Transferred to the ISRO
  • 1980-90 - Responsible for the development of AGNI and PRITHVI Missiles
  • 1981 - Padama Bhusan Government of India
  • 1990 - Padama Vibhushan Government of india
  • 1992-1999 - Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the PM and the Secretary of DRDO
  • 1997 - Bharat Ratna Government of India
  • 2002-2007 - Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2012 - Launched mission for the youth of the nation called "What Can I Give Movement"
  • 2015 - Passed away on 27th July in Shilong Meghalaya
